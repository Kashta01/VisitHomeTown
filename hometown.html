Top 3 places to visit in Al Giza

the Pharaonic Village

Visiting The Pharaonic Village is recommended to be at the beginning of the trip to Egypt.

the Great Pyramid of Giza

Ancient Egyptian pyramid that is the largest of the three Pyramids of Giza, located on a rocky plateau on the west bank of the Nile River in northern Egypt.

the Great Sphinx

The giant statue of the Great Sphinx was built more than 4,500 years ago, to guard the pyramid of Khafre at Giza. It has the head of a pharaoh and the body of a lion.
